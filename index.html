If an emergency situation
3 primary step





Check for danger

Look for signs of danger that could risk your as well as the patients safety, such as any fire, falling debris, or violent people. Then and only then move the patient away, to a safer position.





Call for medical help

If the patient requires medical care, ask a person around you or yourself call 911 or any other local medical emergency servies in your current area.





Provide care

Remain with the patient. Calm them down. Cover them with a warm blanket. If you have any prior knowledge, then provide the patient with first aid till help arrives.

Outlook

While providing first aid, keep your safety in mind. Check your surroundings. Stay away from blood, vomit or any other fluid. Attend to the patient or their wounds using gloves. Use protective gears like breathing barrier if you have to perform rescue breathing for the patient. After you are done, immediately wash your hands.
